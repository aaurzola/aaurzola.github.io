Dream Theater

Dream Theater's members. From left to right: Petrucci, Minneman, Labrie, Myung, Rudess.

    Dream theater's members over time:

  • Formation (1985): Formed in Massachusetts in 1985 when guitarist John Petrucci, bassist John Myung, and drummer Mike Portnoy decided to form a band by the name of 'Majesty'.
  • Lead vocalist and keyboardist (1985-1986): Petrucci asked his high school bandmate Kevin Moore to play the keyboard. Another friend from home, Chris Collins, was recruited as lead vocalist. Nontheless, In November 1986, after a few months of writing and performing together, Chris Collins was fired.
  • 2nd vocalist (1987): After a year of trying to find a replacement, Charlie Dominici, who was far older and more experienced than anyone else in the band, successfully auditioned for the group.
  • 'This is not what we are looking for' (1990): Charlie Dominici was let go because the band was starting to feel the limitations of his voice based upon the vocal style they wanted.
  • Addition of James Labrie (1991): In january 1991, LaBrie jammed on three songs with the band, and was immediately hired to fill the vocalist position.
